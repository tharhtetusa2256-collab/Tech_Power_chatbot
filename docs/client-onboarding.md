# Client Onboarding Checklist

Use this checklist before starting a Tech Power AI automation project.

## 1. Business Information

- Business name:
- Industry:
- Main products or services:
- Target customers:
- Website or Facebook page:
- Contact person:

## 2. Current Problem

Ask the client:

- What tasks take too much time every day?
- What questions do customers ask repeatedly?
- Where do leads currently come from?
- How do you follow up with customers?
- What tools do you already use?

## 3. Automation Opportunity

Choose one starting point:

- FAQ chatbot
- Lead capture bot
- Content assistant
- Google Sheets workflow
- Customer support assistant
- Custom workflow

## 4. Required Assets

Collect:

- Business description
- FAQ list
- Service or product list
- Contact details
- Brand tone
- Example customer messages

## 5. Delivery Plan

- Confirm scope
- Confirm price
- Build prototype
- Test with sample users
- Collect feedback
- Deploy
- Offer monthly maintenance
